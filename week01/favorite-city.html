Arequipa, Peru

Arequipa is my hometown, and for me it is the most beautiful city in the world. It is known as "The White City" because many of its buildings are made from a white volcanic stone called sillar.

The majestic Misti volcano stands tall and seems to watch over the city. Arequipa is also famous for its spicy and delicious food, its kind and friendly people, and the joyful spirit that makes the city special.
